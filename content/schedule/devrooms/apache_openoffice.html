The Apache OpenOffice Devroom is dedicated to the popular open-source office-document productivity suite: Apache OpenOffice and its community and ecosystem.

OpenOffice has been well-known for more than 20 years and it contains six productivity applications based on the OpenDocument Format (ODF): Writer, Calc, Impress, Draw, Math, Base.

In this devroom you will find talks about Apache OpenOffice and related topics: build tools, translation tools, ODF format, community... and more!

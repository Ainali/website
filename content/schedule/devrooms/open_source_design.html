The Open Source Design devroom showcases design work happening within free and open source projects, because yes, there is design live in FOSS. We mean "design" in a broad sense, and we include within that term typography, graphic design, interaction design, user experience, human-computer interaction, accessibility, information architecture, content strategy, service design, design research and <add your favourite buzzword here>.

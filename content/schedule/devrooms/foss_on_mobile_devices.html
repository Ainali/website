The FOSS on mobile devices devroom is a place for development teams around the different existing projects to meet, discuss the latest news and share with an interested audience what they have been working on.

With lots of work being done on the development of FOSS in the mobile space in recent times, more and more devices running (close to) mainline kernels, and improved support for mobile formfactors in various toolkits we invite you to learn how our collective quest for World domination is going!

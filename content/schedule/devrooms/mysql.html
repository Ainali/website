The MySQL & Friends Devroom gives the opportunity to the open source MySQL community to present and discuss innovative development, share experiences and best practices in administering or deploying MySQL servers. Proxies, Clusters, new features, new engines and performances will be covered by the speakers.

This is the best place in Europe where the Community and the professionals meets together. The devroom will end with the traditional Community dinner.

Registration for the dinner is required this year, follow this link to get more information and join the rest of the community for a great evening.

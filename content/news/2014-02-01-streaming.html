--- title: Streaming ---

For the first time ever, FOSDEM is streaming all main tracks and most devrooms at http://streaming.fosdem.org/.

---MORE---

This is on a best-effort basis; in particular, the U building is not streamed. The streams are in WebM format, and should work in most modern browsers and phones. Video distribution is sponsored by Google Compute Engine. Please note that our priority is recording. The streams are best-effort! Nevertheless, should you experience problems, please contact us on #fosdem on IRC. Thanks!

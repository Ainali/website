--- title: Video transcoding progressing ---

Transcoding of our videos is making progress. We're not finished yet, however, and your help is welcome!

---MORE---

There are many transcoded videos already visible on our video archive. However, some videos do have issues, and we have neither the time nor the manpower to review all videos in minute detail.

We would therefore like to ask for some help from the community: if you see some issues with one or more videos on the video archive, it would be helpful if you could inform us.

Examples of problems we've so far encountered include:

  • Misassociation (a video named after one talk actually containing another)
  • Missing audio (image, but no sound, or static rather than sound)
  • Incorrect timings (the talk starts many minutes before or after the start of the video

It's of course also possible that there are other issues which we've not yet identified.

All reports of issues are welcome on the video mailinglist.

FOSDEM 2021 is the first fully online edition of FOSDEM!

Welcome to our first online edition. We still have our familiar programme of keynotes, devrooms and stands, but in a slightly different form. We have collected useful information on how to navigate this brave new world.

Visiting the conference

Keynotes and talks

The full conference can be accessed from the schedule, containing a link to each individual talk or devroom. Each devroom is in a (virtual) room, which you can access by clicking the link. All rooms are hosted on chat.fosdem.org.

Before you can see any video, you will have to click the button (1.) to enable the video button. Virtual room view. 1 is the video window; 2 is the chat window.

The virtual room is where the action happens. Each room is divided in two parts:

  1. The video window, where the stream will be displayed. This will be a combination of a prerecorded video (the talk) and a live recording of the Q&A.
  2. The chat window. You can freely chat (but please keept it relevant and remember our code of conduct). If you find a question very relevant or insightful, you can upvote it by clicking the thumbs up button. The most upvoted questions will be asked during the Q&A at the end of each talk.

It is not necessary to register to watch video's, but you will need an account to post or upvote questions.

Upvoting questions

Click on the emoji face (1.) to upvote a question. After clicking on the emoji face, you'll arrive at a larger window with a lot of emoji's. Clicking on the thumbs up icon (1.) will upvote the question.

You can upvote a question by clicking on the emoji face next to each line. When you click on thumbs up, the question or comment will be upvoted and visible in a special widget for the moderators and hosts. They will decide what questions get asked in the live Q&A.

--- title: Job Corner ---

Businesses will be again able to inform visitors about open source employment or contracting opportunities in our "job corner" at <%= conference[:title] %>.

There are some rules on what is permissible, as outlined below, but most important is to use your common sense.

Allowed

  • Related to open source / free software
  • Open source software development
  • Open source product integration
  • Open source systems administration
  • Flyers with information about the job
  • Passive recruitment (paper) only

Not allowed

  • NO recruitment not related to open source or free software
  • NO partially open source positions
  • NO job adverts on other places than the job corner
  • NO active recruitment (inviting visitors to look at your jobs, handing out flyers or similar)

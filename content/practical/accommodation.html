FOSDEM does not recommend any particular hotel or hostel. However, here is a list of popular accommodations for FOSDEM visitors to get you started. You can also check the mailing list for more information. wikivoyage also lists some places to stay.

Hotels

Brussels has a large selection of hotels in every price range.

FunKey

Like previous year, FunKey Hotel is offering special rates for FOSDEM 2017 attendees. Bookings have been closed for non-FOSDEM attendees, so FOSDEM guests will be able to use the common spaces of the hotel (breakfast room, meeting rooms, ...) at their leisure during their stay.

Their offer includes the following:

  • 28 Euro per bed, per night
  • Breakfast
  • Wireless network
  • 24/7 snacking and beverages (including an extensive selection of beers)
  • Free parking
  • The use of their Chromebook laptops
  • The use of their mobile phones (with unlimited calls in Belgium)

Please note that roughly one third of the rooms have already been booked, so if you are interested, you should book as early as possible.

The offer of 28EUR/bed/night is valid for Friday, Saturday and Sunday night.

Please try to book as groups of 2, 3, or 4 friends or colleagues. It is also possible to book just one bed and share a room with people you don't know.

If you are interested, all you have to do is send an e-mail to Guillaume at guillaume@funkeyhotel.com, indicating:

  • Your name(s)
  • How many beds you wish to book
  • The nights you wish to book

People wishing to book at least 2 nights have priority until 2016-10-01. This means that 1 night only bookings will only be confirmed after this date. Bookings for at least 2 nights can be paid immediately and will be instantly confirmed.

Payment must be made in advance and is nonrefundable. Guillaume will send you details on how to proceed after you have e-mailed him. You will have the possibility of paying either online with Visa, MasterCard or Bancontact (no American Express, Carte Bleue, Visa Electron or others) or by IBAN.

You can learn more about the FunKey hotel here: http://funkeyhotel.com If you have any questions about this offer, please direct them to Guillaume at guillaume@funkeyhotel.com.

Please note that this is an offer from a third party extended to FOSDEM attendees. FOSDEM organisers have no relationship with FunKey. We only forward their offer to our attendees.

CouchSurfing / BeWelcome

Are you looking for a couch or do you maybe have one to offer? Join the FOSDEM CouchSurfing or BeWelcome group and find fellow visitors!

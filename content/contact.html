--- title: Contact ---

Discuss

There is a mailing list (and an archive) to help you get in touch with other FOSDEM visitors.

You may also join us for a chat in the #fosdem channel on freenode.

Help out

Visitors interested in helping out during the event can register for the volunteers mailing list, where all communication between and coordination of volunteers takes place.

In the months before the event, volunteers can register at volunteers.fosdem.org and pick up any of the defined tasks.

Get in touch

You can contact us by email at info@fosdem.org.
